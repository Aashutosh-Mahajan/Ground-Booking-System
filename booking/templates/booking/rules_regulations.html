Ground Booking Rules & Regulations

Follow these rules to ensure safe and fair use of college grounds.

1 Bookings must be made at least 24 hours in advance.
2 Maximum of 2 hours per day per student/group.
3 Only registered students may make bookings.
4 Leave the ground clean and on time.
5 Report any damages immediately.
6 Cancel bookings at least 12 hours in advance.
7 Unauthorized use may lead to suspension.
8 Follow all college safety and sports regulations.
9 Use of alcohol, drugs, or tobacco is strictly prohibited.
10 Maintain proper conduct; do not disturb other groups.
11 Equipment must be used responsibly and returned after use.
12 Restricted areas are off-limits.
13 Only authorized personnel can operate lighting/sound systems.

By using the ground booking system, you agree to follow all rules and regulations.
