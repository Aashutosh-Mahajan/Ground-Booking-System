Ground Booking Rules

Please read and follow these regulations to ensure a safe and fair environment for everyone.

Play Fair, Play Safe

Respect the ground, respect the game.

1

Advance Booking

Bookings must be made at least 24 hours in advance to ensure availability.

2

Time Limit

Maximum of 2 hours per day per student/group to allow fair access for all.

3

Registered Students Only

Only currently registered students with valid ID may make bookings.

4

Cleanliness & Punctuality

Leave the ground clean and vacate strictly on time for the next group.

5

Report Damages

Report any existing damages immediately before starting your game.

6

Cancellation Policy

Cancel bookings at least 12 hours in advance to avoid penalties.

7

Unauthorized Use

Unauthorized use of facilities may lead to suspension of booking privileges.

8

Safety First

Follow all college safety guidelines and sports-specific regulations.

9

Prohibited Items

Use of alcohol, drugs, or tobacco is strictly prohibited on premises.

10

Conduct

Maintain proper conduct; do not disturb other groups or classes nearby.

11

Equipment

College equipment must be used responsibly and returned immediately after use.

12

Restricted Areas

Do not enter restricted areas or maintenance zones.

By using the ground booking system, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to follow all the above rules and regulations.
